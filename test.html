Is australia a good cricket team ?

Australia are the current ICC World Test Championship and ICC Cricket World Cup champions. They are regarded as most successful cricket teams in the history of Cricket.Australia has played England in 356 test matches, with Australia winning 150, England winning 110 and 96 matches being drawn. Test matches are played in series, each series generally 5 matches. In 71 series, Australia has won 33 times, England 32 with 6 drawn series.

Virat Kohli as team captain

Virat led the Indian Cricket Team from 2013 to 2022. He was the captain of the Indian team in all three formats, Test, One Day Internationals and T20 Internationals. He replaced MS Dhoni and carried the baton from there on.Virat Kohli led India in Test cricket in 68 matches. These are the most matches for any Indian captain ever in the longest format. He surpassed MS Dhoni during this period who led India in 60 Tests. Virat Kohli won 40 Test matches as India's Test captain which makes him the most successful for India.
